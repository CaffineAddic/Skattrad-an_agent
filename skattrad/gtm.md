# Go-to-Market Plan — Skattråd

## The market

Sweden has ~400,000 active aktiebolag (AB) and ~250,000 sole traders (enskild firma).
The vast majority are micro-businesses with 1–10 employees who cannot afford a
dedicated revisor or jurist on retainer, yet face the same compliance complexity as
larger companies. A single compliance mistake — wrong VAT declaration, missed
F-skatt registration, incorrect karensavdrag calculation — can cost tens of thousands
of kronor in penalties.

| Segment | Size | Pain level | Willingness to pay |
|---------|------|------------|-------------------|
| Solo founders / frilansare | ~180,000 | 🔴 Very high | $15–30/mo |
| Micro-AB (1–5 employees) | ~150,000 | 🔴 Very high | $30–80/mo |
| Small AB (5–20 employees) | ~70,000 | 🟠 High | $80–200/mo |

**Beachhead:** Solo founders and frilansare. They have the pain, they're active online,
and they make purchase decisions fast without procurement cycles.

---

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)

- **Who:** Swedish freelancer or 1-person AB, 1–3 years in business
- **What they use:** Bokio/Fortnox for accounting, no dedicated revisor
- **Core pain:** Spend 2–4 hours/month on compliance research + still feel uncertain
- **Trigger:** First VAT declaration, first employee, first invoice to EU country
- **Found at:** Facebook groups (Frilansare Sverige), LinkedIn, Företagarna forums, Reddit r/sweden

## Competitive moat

Generic AI (ChatGPT, Gemini) — gives plausible but ungrounded answers, no Swedish-specific sourcing.
Swedish accounting software (Bokio, Fortnox) — handles bookkeeping but not compliance Q&A.
Human revisors — expensive (2,000–5,000 kr/hour), not available on-demand.

**Skattråd's moat:** Always-on, always-sourced, always-Swedish. Every answer links back to
the same sources a revisor would cite. We make the answer *verifiable*, not just plausible.
